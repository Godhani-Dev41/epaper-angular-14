About Us

Welcome to epaperweekly.com, your number one source for all things digital news, delivered to your tablet and specifically designed for the reMarkable paper tablet. We're dedicated to providing you the very best of service, with an emphasis on support, quality, and features.

Founded in 2007 by Jason Smith, Ceigon LLC, Epaperweekly has come a long way from its beginnings in Salt Lake City Utah, USA. When Jason first started out, his passion for "eco-friendly technology solutions" drove them to start their own business. We believe in the direction of e-ink tablets and how it saves the environment. Here at epaperweekly.com we strive to get you off your cell phone and onto an e-ink paper tablet, where you can focus and save the environment at the same time.

We hope you enjoy our services as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
